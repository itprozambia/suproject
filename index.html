The Importance of Oxygen

Medical Oxygen is an essential drug used in the treatment of hypoxemia, pneumonia, COVID-19, emergency resuscitation, anaesthesia and other surgical complications. It’s medical grade range between 93% and 95% purity.

Oxygen Cylinders

These are special containers containing compressed pressured oxygen for patient usage. They use high pressure gauges (connected to another machine-like ventilators, anaesthetic before administering to the patient) and low-pressure gauges when connecting directly to the patient.

Proper oxygen levels ensure that your organs function efficiently and reduce the risk of fatigue and illness.
